# Yardao Privacy Policy

Effective date: [FILL IN DATE]

Yardao ("the app", "we", "us") is operated by [YOUR REGISTERED COMPANY LTD]
("the Company"), a company registered in the United Kingdom. This policy
explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Yardao is a business tool for managing vehicle yards and fleets. Most data in
the app is entered by businesses about their own vehicles and operations.

## Information we collect

Account information
- Name, email address, phone number, and your role within your organisation.
- Authentication details required to sign you in.

Business and operational data you enter
- Vehicle records (registration, make, model, MOT and tax dates, condition,
  mileage, damage notes, photos), service bookings, stock and parts, invoices,
  notes, and customer contact details that you choose to add.

Technical data
- A device push notification token (so we can send you reminders).
- A user identifier used to operate your account.
- Basic diagnostic information needed to keep the service running and secure.

We do not collect your device location for tracking, and we do not use your
data for advertising.

## How we use your information
- To provide the app's features (yard management, servicing, stock, invoicing,
  reminders and notifications).
- To authenticate you and keep your account and organisation's data secure.
- To send service, MOT, booking and reminder notifications you have enabled.
- To respond to support requests.

## Service providers (processors)
We use trusted providers who process data on our behalf, under contract, only
to deliver the service:
- Supabase, for secure database, authentication and hosting.
- Google Firebase Cloud Messaging, to deliver push notifications.
- DVLA and DVSA services, for vehicle lookups (MOT, tax and recall data).
- Groq, to power the in-app assistant when you use it.
- Resend, to send transactional emails.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it with third parties
for their own marketing.

## Data storage and security
Data is stored on secure servers and protected in transit using encryption
(HTTPS/TLS). Access is restricted to your organisation through row level
security so organisations cannot see each other's data.

## Data retention
We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Business records may
be retained where needed to meet legal or accounting obligations.

## Your rights and account deletion
You can delete your account and associated personal data at any time from
within the app: Settings, then your account tab, then "Delete account".
You may also contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of
your personal data. Under UK GDPR you have rights to access, rectify, erase,
restrict and port your data, and to object to processing.

## Children
Yardao is a workplace tool and is not directed to children under 18.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version
with a new effective date.
