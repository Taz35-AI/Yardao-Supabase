# Google Play Data Safety form answers

Use these when filling the Play Console "Data safety" section. They match the
privacy policy. Adjust if you later add analytics or ads (you currently have
neither).

## Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?
Yes (collects). Not shared with third parties for their own purposes.
(Service providers process data on your behalf only.)

## Is all user data encrypted in transit?
Yes.

## Do you provide a way for users to request that their data be deleted?
Yes. Users can delete their account and data in-app
(Settings > account tab > Delete account), and can also email support@yardao.com.

## Data types collected

Personal info
- Name: Collected. Linked to the user. Purpose: App functionality, Account
  management. Not shared. Not for ads.
- Email address: Collected. Linked. Purpose: App functionality, Account
  management. Not shared.
- Phone number: Collected (optional). Linked. Purpose: App functionality.
  Not shared.
- Other info (customer/contact details the user enters about their own
  business contacts): Collected. Linked. Purpose: App functionality.

App activity / Other user-generated content
- Vehicle records, service bookings, stock, invoices, notes: Collected.
  Linked. Purpose: App functionality. Not shared.

Photos
- Vehicle/condition photos the user attaches: Collected. Linked. Purpose:
  App functionality. Not shared. (Only if the user adds photos.)

App info and performance
- Diagnostics / crash logs: Collected (for reliability). Not linked to identity
  where possible. Purpose: App functionality. Not shared.
  (Set to "not collected" if you ship without any crash/analytics SDK.)

Device or other identifiers
- Push notification token / user ID: Collected. Linked. Purpose: App
  functionality (notifications, account). Not shared.

## Data NOT collected
- Precise or approximate device location (the app does not track user location).
- Financial/payment info (no in-app payments).
- Contacts, calendar, SMS, browsing history, health, advertising data.
